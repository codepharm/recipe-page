Spinach Omlette

20 min Prep Time
300 grams Egg whites
1 whole Large Egg
60 grams Spinach
15 grams Mozzerrella
10 grams Mustard
10 grams Sriracha
1
Preheat stove-top to medium low heat, add non-stick spray if necessary. Chop spinach, add salt & pepper, season to taste. Preheat toaster oven to broil and position rack to the top position.
2
Add whole egg and egg whites to the seasoned spinach. Mix well and add to the pre-heated pan. Cook for 2 mins, add mustard and sriracha to the top of the omlette.
Cook for another 5 mins, or until egg whites are cooked through. Add mozzarella to the top of the omlette and place in the toaster oven for 2 mins, or until cheese is melted. Serve with toast and enjoy!
3
